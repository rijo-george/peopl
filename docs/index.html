What makes it different

🌤️ Relationship Weather

Each person gets a weather icon based on how recently you've been in touch. Sunny to stormy — know at a glance who needs attention.

🎂 Birthdays & Dates

Never miss a birthday. The "Coming Up" section shows all important dates in the next two weeks.

⚡ Keyboard First

Add a person [A], log an interaction [I], navigate with j/k. Everything is one keystroke away.

☁️ iCloud Sync

One JSON file. Syncs across Mac and iOS via iCloud Drive. No account, no server, no tracking.

🎨 6 Themes

Dark, light, sunset, ocean, forest, rose. Switch with [T] or press 1-6.

📋 Interaction Log

Coffee, call, email, text, meeting. Log what happened with who and when. Build a history.

No account. No cloud service. No tracking. Just a local app that helps you remember the people in your life. Your data stays on your devices.
