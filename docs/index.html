What's inside

💌 Nudge Cards

Open the app and it tells you who needs attention. Call, text, or skip — one tap away.

⚡ Quick Capture

Type "priya - loves pottery" and it's saved. Voice memos too. Always-visible bar at the bottom.

📚 Living Timeline

Memories and interactions woven into one stream, grouped by month. Your shared history at a glance.

📋 Person Details

Food, music, movies, hobbies, allergies — 20+ suggested fields plus custom ones. Know what makes them tick.

🌤️ Relationship Weather

Each person gets a weather icon. Sunny to stormy — know at a glance who needs love.

🌠 Surfaced Memories

"1 year ago — about James: He brought homemade sourdough..." Old memories bubble up to surprise you.

🎨 7 Themes

Journal, dark, light, sunset, ocean, forest, rose. Each with warm colors that feel alive.

☁️ iCloud Sync

One JSON file. Syncs via iCloud Drive. No account, no server, no tracking. Your data stays yours.
